Overview of Design Section:

Data visualization using wireframes is the most important part of designing and creating a website. it is the foundation of all that will be done and performed on the website.

I've had to also re-evaluate my strategy when it comes to designing this webpage. As I have placed too much emphasis on aesthetics and design rather than effeiciency and functionality. I have taken a step back from that approach and went for a design that is functionally sound and working with all the links leading to the designated pages and the implementation of the API function.

To be honest I have struggled with applying the techniques taught in lectures. Thus I previously compensated by adding more aesthetics which lead to clutter and unreadibility. I am learning to first apply the basics and build from there as I have down in this iteration.
